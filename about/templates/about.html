About

We started The Irish Cookie Store in November 2020 and are based in Waterford City! We make American style cookies that weigh a minimum of 175g with creative flavors ad mix-ins. Our flavors can be mixed and matched! Our cookies are different to most other cookies on the Irish market as they are not flat, they are thick stuffed cookies!
